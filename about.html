About the Author

Christian has a background as a researcher in mathematical physics and holds a PhD from the Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm. After his physics career, Christian worked for a few years on designing algorithms for internet traffic. Thereafter, he turned to investment banking and worked for Credit Suisse, Lehman Brothers, SEB, and Handelsbanken.

Christian possesses diverse expertise and has authored scientific articles and books across various disciplines such as physics, mathematics, finance, information technology, philosophy, and biology.
